WHO AM I?

I’m Joshua Lauren T. Villaruel, a first-year Entertainment and Multimedia Computing – Game Development student at CIIT College of Arts and Technology. I’m passionate about creating interactive digital experiences and exploring how creativity and technology merge to bring ideas to life.

My interest in game development comes from a natural curiosity about systems, mechanics, and how each decision impacts a player’s experience. I enjoy understanding how things work, breaking down complex concepts, and building solutions that are functional, intuitive, and engaging.

Outside game development, I work on a wide range of projects—from UI/UX concepts and productivity tools to automation scripts and academic research. I value continuous growth and enjoy learning deeply, especially when it comes to building skills that help me become a better problem-solver and developer.

Right now, I’m focused on strengthening my foundations, developing meaningful projects, and moving step by step toward becoming a professional Game Developer.
