MY HOBBIES

Pursuits and Passions

Archive Aesthetic Identity

I am fascinated by **vintage digital interfaces, early web design, and media archiving**. This hobby involves curating digital collections, experimenting with retro operating systems (through emulation or virtual machines), and appreciating the history and evolution of computer graphics and user experience. It's about preserving and remixing the past in a modern context.

Tinkering & Thrift Finds

My practical side enjoys **repairing, modifying, and repurposing old electronics**. Finding functional hardware at thrift stores or flea markets provides a rewarding challenge. This hands-on activity involves circuit board diagnostics, soldering practice, and transforming discarded tech into usable, sometimes unconventional, devices.

Space for Friends and Gaming

Gaming is always better with community. I dedicate time to **collaborative online gaming** with friends and maintaining digital spaces (like Discord servers) where we can connect, share ideas, and relax. This focus on multiplayer experiences reinforces teamwork, communication, and community building, skills I value highly in project work.

These interests drive my technical curiosity and provide a balanced perspective outside of formal studies.
