MY CAREER JOURNEY

"Ultimately, my career path reflects both my personal growth and my commitment to pursuing work that aligns with my values, interests, and strengths."

The Engineering Origins

Growing up, I originally envisioned myself as an engineer, specifically in the field of mechanical engineering. The idea of designing machines, understanding mechanics, and solving complex technical problems excited me. I spent time imagining the projects I could build and the innovations I could contribute to the world.

The Turning Point

However, as I progressed, I experienced burnout. The workload, the pressure, and the disconnect between my day-to-day experiences and my personal passions began to take a toll. I realized that while engineering fascinated me on paper, it no longer aligned with what I truly wanted to dedicate my energy to.

This led me to reflect on my ongoing passion: game development. From the earliest days of experimenting with Minecraft Redstone to tinkering with automation scripts and designing UI/UX layouts, I discovered that creating interactive digital experiences is where I feel most engaged, curious, and motivated. Game development combines creativity, technical problem-solving, and structured thinking in a way that feels natural to me.

The Goal: Professional Development

Today, my career goal is clear: I want to become a professional Game Developer, working on projects that challenge me, excite me, and allow me to grow.

I aim to contribute to projects that inspire others, challenge conventions, and reflect the blend of creativity and logic that has always drawn me toward game development.
